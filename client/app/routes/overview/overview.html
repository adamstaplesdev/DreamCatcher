Dream Catcher Overview

Dream Catcher is a website dedicated to allowing you the freedom to manage your dreams in a way that is free, easy, and convenient. Dream Catcher allows the creation of dreams which are made up of goals. Collectively these goals help you accomplish your dream! It is completely customizable to fit all of your dreams!

Click below to create your first dream today!

Dream Catcher Dreams

Dream Catcher is primarily made up of dreams, created by you! You decide what your dream is. Each dream is made up of goals that you will also create to allow you to monitor the progress you have made towards completing your dream!

Dream Catcher Goals

Every dream that you create is made up of goals. Each goal marks progress you make towards accomplishing your dream. To further customize each goal, you are also able to create subgoals, to monitor progress you make towards reaching your goal. You can fully customize the progress you make towards accomplishing your dream by adding subgoals to any of your goals.

Dream Catcher Groups

Dream Catcher not only allows you to create new dreams but it also allows you to share those dreams with other users. Each user that is part of the group can see the dreams of everyone else and see the progress that everyone has made. This can be especially useful for creating groups for your family and friends.
